How to use our website Code Champions?

| Create an Account

Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button.

Fill in your details, including first name, surname, email, and password.

Click "Submit" to create your account.

| Log In

If you already have an account, click on the "Log In" button.

Enter your email and password.

Click "Log In" to access your account.

| Explore Exercises

Navigate to the "Exercises" section to find a variety of programming challenges.

Click on a specific exercise to view its details.

| Profile and Statistics

Visit the "Profile" section to see your user information and rankings.

Explore the "Statistics" page for an overview of your solved problems and difficulty distribution.

| Toggle Profile State

In your profile, use the toggle button to switch between "On" and "Off" states.

This feature may control the visibility or privacy of your profile.

| Help Section

Check the "Help" section for any additional assistance or FAQs.

| Recent Submissions

The "Recent Submissions" section shows your latest code submissions and their outcomes.

| Activity Calendar

In the "Activity Calendar," visualize your programming activity over the past year.

| Enjoy Learning

Engage with coding challenges, track your progress, and enjoy the learning experience on our platform!
